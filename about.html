About SouthGrid Studio

We are a premium web design & development agency based in Jebel Suk, Juba. Specialized in helping construction companies and local businesses dominate digital space.

Our Mission

To empower South Sudanese businesses with world-class websites that drive real growth, innovation, and digital leadership.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted digital partner in East Africa for construction, trade, and enterprise sectors.

Riek Gatluak — Founder & Lead Architect

Riek Gatluak is a visionary web developer and digital strategist with over 10 years of experience crafting high-performance websites for businesses across Africa. Born and raised in Juba, Riek understood early the power of digital presence for local enterprises. After earning a degree in Computer Science and working with international agencies, he returned to South Sudan with a mission: to help construction companies, logistics firms, and local brands compete globally through exceptional web experiences.

Riek has led projects for major construction firms, built scalable e-commerce platforms, and trained dozens of developers. His approach combines technical excellence with a deep understanding of business goals, ensuring every website doesn't just look beautiful but also converts visitors into loyal clients. Under his leadership, SouthGrid Studio has become the go-to agency for premium web solutions in Juba.
